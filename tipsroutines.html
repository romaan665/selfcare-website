Daily, Weekly, and Monthly Self-Care Checklists

  • Take a 10-minute morning walk
  • Drink enough water
  • Schedule a weekly "me-time" session
  • Declutter your workspace monthly
  • Practice gratitude journaling

Morning & Night Routines

Morning: Start your day with a warm cup of tea, stretch for 5 minutes, and review your goals.

Night: Turn off screens 30 minutes before bed, read a calming book, and reflect on your day.

Mental Health Tips

Manage stress by practicing mindfulness for 5 minutes daily. Take deep breaths, count to ten, and focus on the present. Avoid over-scheduling, and remember to seek help when feeling overwhelmed.
